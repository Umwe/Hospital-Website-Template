Cardiology

Heart and Veins related Diseases

Cardiology is the medical specialty that focuses on the study, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases and conditions related to the cardiovascular system, which includes the heart and blood vessels. Physicians who specialize in cardiology are called cardiologists. The cardiovascular system is crucial for maintaining the circulation of blood throughout the body, delivering oxygen and nutrients to organs and tissues.

Neurology

Brain and Central Nervous System Disorders

Neurology is a medical specialty that focuses on the study, diagnosis, and treatment of disorders and diseases of the nervous system. The nervous system includes the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, and the neuromuscular junction. Physicians who specialize in neurology are called neurologists.

Hepatology

Internal muscle diagnosis(Liver)

Hepatology is a medical specialty that focuses on the study, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases and conditions related to the liver, gallbladder, biliary tree, and pancreas. The medical professionals who specialize in hepatology are known as hepatologists. The liver is a vital organ with numerous functions, including detoxification of substances in the body, production of bile for digestion, and storage of nutrients.

Pediatrics

Diagnosis and Treatment of Childhood Illnesses

Pediatrics is a branch of medicine that focuses on the healthcare of infants, children, and adolescents. ] It involves the medical care and well-being of individuals from birth through adolescence, generally up to the age of 18. Physicians who specialize in pediatrics are called pediatricians.

Ophthalmology

Treatment of Eye Injuries.

Ophthalmology is a medical specialty that deals with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases and disorders related to the eyes and the visual system. Physicians who specialize in ophthalmology are known as ophthalmologists. They are trained to provide comprehensive eye care, from routine eye examinations to surgical interventions.
